About Us

We are a team of passionate weather enthusiasts, developers, and educators who came together to build the Snow Day Calculator. Our mission is simple: to help students, teachers, and parents quickly and easily predict snow days, so they can plan their schedules accordingly. We believe that technology should make everyday life easier, and we're excited to provide this fun and useful tool for the community.
