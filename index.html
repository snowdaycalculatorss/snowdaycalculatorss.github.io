What Is the Snow Day Calculator?

The Snow Day Calculator is an online tool that predicts the likelihood of school being closed due to snow. You enter your zip code, school type (public, private, etc.), and a few other details, such as being a student or teacher. Then, it gives you a percentage chance that school will be closed the next day.

About Us

We’re a group of weather enthusiasts who built this tool to help people predict snow days and plan their days accordingly.
